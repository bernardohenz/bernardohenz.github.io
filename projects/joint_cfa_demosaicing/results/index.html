This is the supplementary material for the paper Deep Joint Design of Color Filter Arrays and Demosaicing, presented in Eurographics 2018 and published in a special issue of Computer Graphics Forum. We show additional reconstructions, comparing our method for noise-free and noisy scenarios.

The images below are a random sample from the moiré and vdp datasets [GCPD16]. Best PSNR values shown in bold.

Important: These materials are best viewed in a web browser that is not Internet Explorer (IE), since it cannot resize images properly.

Please click on any image to see them in a larger size, then use the arrow keys from your keyboard for navigating between the images.
